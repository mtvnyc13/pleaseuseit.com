üß≠ Quick How-To Guides

  • Make a DoorDash Dasher account (10‚Äì20 min)
    1. Open dasher.doordash.com and enter email/phone.
    2. Upload ID and consent to a background check.
    3. Choose vehicle type (car, scooter, bike).
    4. Set up direct deposit (see Money).
    5. Complete any activation steps and start dashing.
  • Start Uber Eats delivery
    1. Go to uber.com/deliver and sign in or create account.
    2. Upload ID, vehicle docs (if car), and pass background check.
    3. Add bank info for payouts.
    4. Complete onboarding; pick up your first order.
  • Get paid: set up Direct Deposit
    1. Open your banking app (e.g., Chime, Cash App, Venmo).
    2. Find Direct Deposit ‚Äî copy routing & account numbers.
    3. Paste into the gig app (DoorDash/Uber) or give to your employer.
    4. Test with a small transfer to confirm.
  • Build credit from scratch
    1. Open a builder (Self or Kikoff) and make on-time payments.
    2. Use Experian Boost to add bills.
    3. Pull free credit report and monitor monthly.
  • Generate a resume with ChatGPT
    1. Open ChatGPT.
    2. Paste this prompt: "Write a one-page resume for entry-level delivery/warehouse/hospitality based on these details: [paste your info]. Use bullet points, keep it simple."
    3. Copy the result into Google Docs and export to PDF.

Miami Quick-Start Guide

  1. Call 211 for local help; for crisis dial 988.
  2. Get shelter: Camillus House / Miami Rescue Mission / Salvation Army.
  3. Apply for SNAP/Medicaid via MyACCESS Florida.
  4. Open bank (Chime) and enable Direct Deposit.
  5. Start income fast: DoorDash / Uber Eats / Instacart.
  6. Begin credit: Self or Kikoff; add bills with Experian Boost.
  7. Replace ID: SSA card, birth certificate, then Florida ID.
  8. Use libraries for Wi-Fi/charging and printing.

Important Contacts & Notes

  • Homeless Helpline: 1-877-994-4357
  • 211 Miami ‚Äî Food/Shelter/Services: 211 or 305-631-4211
  • Legal Aid: Legal Services of Greater Miami; Christian Legal Aid (see site)
